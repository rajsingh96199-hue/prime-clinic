FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our clinic and services.

What are the clinic working hours?

Prime Clinic is open Monday through Saturday from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM. We also provide 24/7 emergency services for urgent medical needs. Sunday appointments are available for emergencies only.

Do I need to book an appointment in advance?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend booking an appointment in advance to minimize waiting time and ensure you get your preferred time slot. You can book online through our website or call us directly.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit and debit cards, UPI payments, and most health insurance plans. We also offer flexible payment plans for long-term treatments. Please contact our billing department for more details.

Do you have in-house laboratory and pharmacy?

Yes, Prime Clinic features a fully equipped in-house laboratory for blood tests, ECG, and other diagnostic services. We also have an in-house pharmacy for your convenience, ensuring you can get your medications immediately after your consultation.

Do you provide emergency services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency support. Dr. Vasant Sawant has been known to respond to emergency calls even in the middle of the night (1 AM to 4 AM) and arrive within minutes to start immediate treatment.

What specialties do your doctors cover?

Our team covers General Medicine, Cardiology, Pediatrics, Dermatology, Dental Care, and Laboratory Diagnostics. We also have specialized care for diabetes management and day care facilities.
